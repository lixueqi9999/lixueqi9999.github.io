About

I'm a Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science in University of Illinois, Urbana Champaign, working with Professor Indranil Gupta in the Distributed Protocols Research Group (DPRG). My research interests mainly includes all aspects of distributed system, and I am now working on the multi-tendancy of graph processing system. My CV is shown here.

Before joining UIUC, I completed my undergraduate study in Shanghai Jiao Tong University, majored in Computer Science and Technology.
